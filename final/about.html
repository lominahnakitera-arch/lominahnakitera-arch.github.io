About Us

Youth Heaven is a youth-centered organization committed to creating safe, inclusive, and empowering spaces for young people across Uganda.

We support youth through mentorship, leadership training, disability inclusion, advocacy, spiritual development, and life skills programs that help them grow into responsible and confident leaders.

Our mission is to empower young people, especially youth with disabilities and marginalized groups, to thrive socially, emotionally, spiritually, and economically.

Our Core Values

  • Respect & Dignity
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
